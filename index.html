About the property

A family home,
shared with you

Varad Yuthi is a family-owned farmhouse in the serene village of Ambavne, just minutes from the heart of Lonavla — with a direct view of Korigad Fort from the property.

Whether you're here for Bhushi Dam, treks to Korigad, the famous chikki, or simply Lonavla's cool misty air — our home is your ideal base.
